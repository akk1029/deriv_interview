Our standard return policy allows customers to return any product within 30 days of the original purchase date for a full refund. To be eligible for a return, the item must be unused, in the same condition that you received it, and in its original packaging with all tags attached. Please note that certain clearance items are marked as final sale and cannot be returned or exchanged. For electronic items, we enforce a stricter 14-day return window due to rapid depreciation. Shipping costs for returns are the responsibility of the customer unless the product arrived damaged.
