We offer free standard shipping on all domestic orders over $50. For orders under $50, a flat shipping fee of $5.99 applies. Standard shipping orders are typically processed within 1 to 2 business days, and delivery takes between 3 to 5 business days depending on your location. If you need your items sooner, we offer an expedited overnight shipping option for a flat rate of $25.00, provided the order is placed before 2:00 PM EST Monday through Friday. We do not currently ship to international addresses or P.O. boxes.
