All of our premier products come with a limited 1-year manufacturer warranty starting from the date of purchase. This warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship under normal use. It does not cover accidental damage, cosmetic wear and tear, spills, misuse, or modifications made by unauthorized repair centers. If a valid claim is filed within the warranty period, we will either repair the product using new parts or replace the unit with a refurbished model of equal value. To initiate a warranty claim, please email support with your original order number and a photo of the defect.
